Substrate Preparation

Speed Without Compromise

MMA's rapid cure doesn't mean shortcuts on preparation. Surface prep follows the same rigorous standards as epoxy systems.

  • Shot blasting — Preferred method for optimal surface profile. Can proceed immediately after
  • Diamond grinding — Alternative where shot blasting isn't feasible
  • Moisture tolerance — More forgiving than epoxy, but excess moisture still requires management
  • Cold conditions — No heating required for sub-zero installations. Concrete can be as cold as -30°C
  • Rapid primer cure — MMA primers cure in 30-60 minutes, enabling same-day system completion

Technology

MMA Chemistry

01

Reactive Primer

MMA-based primer penetrates and seals substrate. Cures in 30-60 minutes at 20°C, proportionally faster at higher temperatures.

02

Body Coat

Self-leveling or broadcast MMA resin. Aggregate broadcast for slip resistance if required. Cures in 1-2 hours.

03

Seal Coat

Clear or pigmented topcoat. UV-stable aliphatic formulations for exterior or high-light applications. Full cure in 1-2 hours.

System Variants

Self-Leveling

Smooth, seamless finish. 2-3mm. Rapid installation for minimal downtime.

Quartz Broadcast

Colored quartz for durability and slip resistance. 3-4mm total build.

Anti-Slip

Textured aggregate finish for wet areas, ramps, and exterior applications.

Decorative Flake

Color flake broadcast for aesthetic appeal. Common in retail and commercial.

Advantages

Why Choose MMA

Rapid Cure

Full traffic in 2-4 hours. Complete projects in a single shift or overnight.

Cold Temperature Cure

Installs at -30°C to +35°C. No heating required for cold storage or winter work.

Durability & Maintenance

MMA Floor Care

MMA floors deliver performance comparable to epoxy with significantly faster installation. Maintenance is straightforward.

Daily

Sweep or auto-scrub to remove debris. Spot clean spills as they occur.

Weekly

Machine scrub with neutral pH cleaner. Rinse thoroughly.

Quarterly

Deep clean and inspection. Check high-wear areas for topcoat condition.

As Needed

Rapid recoating possible — MMA topcoat can be applied with minimal prep.
