FAQ

Common Questions

What information do you need for a quote?

For an accurate quote, we need: floor area (m²), current substrate condition, intended use of the space, desired aesthetic, and timeline. Site photos are helpful.

Do you offer site assessments?

Yes. For projects over 50m², we provide complimentary on-site substrate evaluation and system recommendations.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timeline depends on the system and area. Most residential projects (50-150m²) complete in 3-7 days. Industrial projects may require 1-3 weeks.

Do you work with architects and designers?

We regularly collaborate with architects and interior designers. We provide technical specifications, material samples, and can attend design meetings.
