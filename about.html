Our Approach

What Drives Us

Technical Excellence

Every project begins with thorough substrate analysis and system specification. We match the right floor system to your specific environment and performance requirements.

Our Story

Built on Expertise

Floor.DSGN was founded by flooring engineers who saw a gap in the Israeli market: a need for truly technical flooring solutions delivered with architectural sensibility.

Today, we serve industrial facilities, commercial developers, architects, and homeowners who demand performance without compromising aesthetics. From factory epoxy to handcrafted terrazzo, we deliver floors that work.

Our team combines deep material science knowledge with hands-on installation expertise. We don't just sell systems — we engineer solutions.
